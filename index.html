About iSmart Digital Connect

iSmart Digital Connect was founded by Idris Bashorun, a Lagos-born tech enthusiast, in October 2023. Idris started at 17, building multiple solutions before achieving traction with Rewarder, a campus ticketing app.

Our mission is to revolutionize the tech space by creating apps and websites that solve real problems, empower creators, and make digital content accessible worldwide.

Pose is our first global product, designed to help creators earn fairly while giving users flexible and affordable access to premium content.
